# Peruvian Mountain Rides

A premier adventure tourism company that connects mountain biking enthusiasts with Peru's spectacular Andes mountains through expertly guided expeditions. Peruvian Mountain Rides offers unforgettable high-altitude adventures while supporting sustainable tourism and local communities.

## Project Overview

Peruvian Mountain Rides was born from a passion for Peru's incredible mountain terrain and a desire to share these spectacular landscapes with adventure-seeking mountain bikers from around the world. Founded by local riders who know every trail, stream crossing, and scenic viewpoint in the Sacred Valley region.

Named after the Peruvian Andes that serve as our playground, this company focuses on creating authentic, high-quality mountain biking experiences that respect local culture while delivering world-class adventure tourism.

## Key Features

### Sacred Valley Adventures
- Epic single-track descents through ancient Incan landscapes
- Technical trails ranging from beginner-friendly to expert-level challenges
- Breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks and traditional Andean villages
- Access to exclusive trails known only to local riders

### High Altitude Expeditions
- Mountain biking at elevations exceeding 12,000 feet above sea level
- Specialized high-altitude preparation and safety protocols
- Acclimatization programs for international visitors
- Professional-grade equipment designed for extreme conditions

### Cultural Immersion
- Authentic encounters with local Quechua communities
- Traditional Andean meals prepared by local families
- Educational experiences about Incan history and modern indigenous culture
- Opportunities to support local artisans and community projects

### Professional Guiding Services
- Expert local guides with intimate knowledge of the terrain
- Comprehensive safety protocols and emergency response training
- Multi-lingual guide services (Spanish, English, Quechua)
- Small group sizes ensuring personalized attention and safety

### Sustainable Tourism Practices
- Direct partnerships with local communities ensuring economic benefits
- Environmental conservation initiatives protecting mountain ecosystems
- Leave No Trace principles strictly enforced on all expeditions
- Support for local trail maintenance and conservation projects

## Adventure Packages

### Sacred Valley Classic (3 Days)
- Beginner to intermediate single-track trails
- Cultural visits to traditional Andean villages
- Professional guide and equipment included

### Inca Trail Technical Challenge (5 Days)

- High-altitude acclimatization program
